Volunteering and Internship

Summer Tech Live, 2019

Completed internship with West Gippsland Healthcare Group in January 2019 through the Victorian Government's Summer Tech Live program.

Designed and built data warehouse for Warragul Hospital to match maternity data from source system with related patient data from other areas of the hospital. Will be used for analysis to develop forecasting model for emergency ceasarian births.

Binary Shift, 2018

Volunteered at Binary Shift Regional Innovation & Startups Conference in 2018, including marketting/promoting event and general on-hand assistance throughout the conference.

Assisted attendees with use of Virtual Reality headsets for 360° videos and mobile devices for viewing of Augemented Reality art exhibit by artist Marc-O-Matic.

Fed Uni Games and Tech Society

Elected President of FedUni Games and Tech Society (formerly FedUni IT Society) in 2018. Student society aimed at encouraging student engagement on campus through social events and providing information on tech related events and opportunities in the local area.

Responsible for overseeing and directing the society including finances, events, partnerships and promotion.
